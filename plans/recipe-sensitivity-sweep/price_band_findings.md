Pairs analysed: **13846** (one per agent×pair open).

## Method

- Bin opens by price into 6 bands.
- Within each band, split pairs into quartiles by
  `max(direction_back_prob, direction_lay_prob)` at placement.
- Compute the % of pairs that ended up `matured` in each quartile.
- **Lift** = top-quartile mat% ÷ bottom-quartile mat%. >1 means
  the predictor is informative; ~1 means it's not.
