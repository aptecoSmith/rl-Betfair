Garage

Models saved for future re-evaluation or deployment. Garaged models survive Start Afresh.

Garaged models are not included in the training population. They sit out of evolution entirely. When a training run finishes, you can optionally re-evaluate garaged models on the latest test data using the "Re-evaluate garaged models" checkbox on the Training page. This lets you compare your saved favourites against newly evolved agents without retraining them.
