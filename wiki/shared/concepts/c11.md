# C11 — direction head architecture

Winner of the [[direction-head-architecture-sweep]]. Architecture:

```
LayerNorm → Linear(23, 256) → ReLU → Linear(256, 128) → ReLU → Linear(128, 2)
```

Trained with **pos_weight=1** (unweighted [[bce]]). Default recipe: Adam, lr=1e-3, batch=4096, 50 epochs, patience=5.

## What it is

- Combines the round-2 wider+deeper winner (C9 = `[256, 128]`, balanced) with the round-1 calibration finding (C3/C8 = unweighted BCE).
- Best mean Pearson (+0.2921), lowest mean Brier (0.1433), AUC tied with deeper-balanced variants.
- Calibrated: pred mean tracks empirical positive rate to within 1-2 percentage points on every eval day.
- Consistent across all 10 held-out days — leads or ties on 9/10.

## Why it matters

C11 is the head manifest that should ship into the next cohort relaunch:
```
--direction-head-manifest models/direction_head/sweep_c11
```
With C11 in place the `direction_gate_threshold` gene becomes *meaningful* — the head's "25% chance" actually corresponds to ~25% empirical. Pre-C11 the threshold was nominally a probability but actually a rank order.
